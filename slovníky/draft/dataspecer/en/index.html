Popis
The date, time, or a combination of date and time can be used to represent moments in time. If it is clear which data type is appropriate at a given location, the specific data type, i.e. date, time or date and time, shall be used. Often, however, when creating an OFN, it is not known in advance what level of detail will be available for the determination of the instant. To avoid different representations of this situation in different OFNs, a Time Moment class is specified.
